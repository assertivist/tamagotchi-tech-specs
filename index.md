## Introduction

[tamagotch.org](http://tamagotch.org) is a project that aims at documenting the technical specifications of Tamagotchi toys.

Versions: P1, P2, Angel and Ocean.

Versions: V1, V2 and V3, V4, V4.5, V5, V5.5 and V6.
